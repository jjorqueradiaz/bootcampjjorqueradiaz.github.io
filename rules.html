Rules of The Game and Policies

FIFA rules will govern the game of NYSL except as modified in this document

Sportsmanship

  • The common interest shared by the members of the Association is to inspire
    young people to practice the ideals of sportsmanship and fair play.
    Any player, coach, team, parent, spectator, administrator or referee whose
    behavior detracts from this purpose is subject to disciplinary action
    regardless of soccer technical background, experience,achievements or position.


FIFA Field Rules

  1. Dimensions

    FIFA Law 1 provides for field dimensions flexible external within maximum
    and minimum width and lengthgiven. Dimensions must be met for all fields
    used by teams under 12 years of age or older.


  2. Fields of Competence

    The fields used within the programcompetition
    must be a minimum of 100 x 60 yards. The leagues, districts or associations participating
    in the state competition program who cannot provide a field that meets these requirements
    minimums must advise the State Competition Board that will assign your Field Committee
    to inspect the field and recommend to the Board if must grant or not an exemption
    from the minimum dimensions Teams leagues, districts, or associations that cannot
    provide a field acceptable they may be required to play all their games away.


  3. Fields used by young age groups

    1. U-6 plays on a field of approximately 20 X 40 yards without penalty areas.Fields
      for older age groups they should be progressively larger.

    2. The U-8 plays on a field 40-50 yards long and 20-30 yards Wide.

    3. U-10 plays on a field 70-80 yards long and 40-50 yards long width.

    4. Also, the fields used by the young age groups can have its internal
      dimensions and the size of the target similarly modified.


      Everybody Plays

      • Everybody plays means every recreational player on every team plays
        at least fifty percent of each game. It is encouraged to coaches in the competition
        program to play their players on 50% of each game.


        • A Coach shall be allowed not to play a player 50% of a play in special circumstances,
          for example absences unjustified practices and / or games , rude conduct in
          practice and / or games, and injuries.
